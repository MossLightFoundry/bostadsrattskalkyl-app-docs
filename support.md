`Bostadsrättskalkyl` support

If you need help, want to report a bug, or want to suggest an improvement, contact:

- `mosslightfoundry@gmail.com`

## Helpful information to include

When contacting support, it helps if you include:

- your device model
- whether you used iPhone or web
- your iOS version or browser version
- a short description of the issue
- steps to reproduce it
- Note: do not include any personal data such as financial data

## About the app

`Bostadsrättskalkyl` helps users estimate monthly housing costs for Swedish tenant-owner apartments, compare scenarios, and stress-test interest-rate changes.

This app provides estimates only and does not constitute financial advice.

## Privacy

Your financial inputs stay on your device and are not transmitted to external servers.

The app does not collect or transmit personal data outside your device.

Read the privacy policy here:

- ` https://MossLightFoundry.github.io/bostadsrattskalkyl-app-docs/privacy-policy`
