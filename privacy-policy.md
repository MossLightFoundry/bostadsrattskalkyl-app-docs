# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

`Bostadsrättskalkyl` is an app for estimating monthly housing costs when buying a Swedish tenant-owner apartment.

We value your privacy.

## What information the app uses

The app lets you enter financial information such as:

- income
- purchase price
- down payment
- mortgage assumptions
- monthly association fee
- operating costs
- living costs
- comparison scenarios

## Disclaimer

The app provides estimates and simulations for informational purposes only. It does not constitute financial advice. You should consult a qualified financial advisor before making financial decisions.

We do not guarantee the accuracy or completeness of calculations, which are based on simplified assumptions.

## How your information is stored

- All data entered in the app is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers by the developer.
- We do not have access to your financial inputs.
- The app does not require an account.
- The app does not collect or transmit personal data outside your device.

## How your information is used

The information you enter is used only to:

- calculate housing costs
- compare affordability scenarios
- show stress-test outcomes inside the app

## Analytics and tracking

- The app does not use advertising trackers.
- The app does not sell your personal data.
- If analytics or crash reporting are added in the future, this policy will be updated before that data collection starts.

## Third-party services

The app does not send your financial data to third-party services. Platform providers such as Apple may process technical data necessary for app distribution (e.g., downloads or crash logs), but this is outside of our control.

## Your choices

- All data is deleted when the app is removed from your device.
- You should take care when using the app on shared devices.

If you export your data, you are responsible for how and where that data is stored or shared.

## Legal basis

The app processes data locally on your device solely to provide its functionality (cost calculations and scenario simulations). No personal data is processed by us outside of the user’s device.
